Why are you interested in User Experience Design?

In my experience as a visual designer I have learned that human are very simple, and with that comes low tolerance for complex situations.

My mission has always to push the limits of design with humans at the forefront. As technology changes, humans need to adapt quickly and my goal is to make that process seamless as if doesn't exist at all.
